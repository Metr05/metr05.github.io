[f(x)+g(x])′=f′(x)+g′(x)[f(x)+g(x])'=f'(x)+g'(x) [f(x)+g(x])′=f′(x)+g′(x)

  1. 与常数的乘积法则

[Cf(x)]′=Cf′(x)[Cf(x)]'=Cf'(x) [Cf(x)]′=Cf′(x)

  1. 乘积法则

[f(x)⋅g(x)]′=f′(x)g(x)+g′(x)f(x)[f(x)\cdot g(x)]'=f'(x)g(x)+g'(x)f(x) [f(x)⋅g(x)]′=f′(x)g(x)+g′(x)f(x)

[f(x)g(x)]′=f′(x)g(x)−f(x)g′(x)g2(x)[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}]'=\frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{g^2(x)} [g(x)f(x)​]′=g2(x)f′(x)g(x)−f(x)g′(x)​

  1. 链式法则

y′(x)=y′(z)z′(x)y'(x)=y'(z)z'(x) y′(x)=y′(z)z′(x)
