情态动词与虚拟语气

情态动词的用法

can/could, be able to

  1. 表示能力（could是can的过去式）

  2. 表示请求和允许, could语气更委婉, 但回答必须用can

  3. 表示理论上的可能性, “有时会”（用在肯定句中）

    Always believe that good things are possible, and remember that mistakes can be lessons that lead to discoveries.

  4. 表示推测“可能”（用于否定句、疑问句）

    This can’t br his car, because his is being repaired in the garage.

  5. 固定结构

    cannot … too/enough 无论…也不过分；越…越好

    cannot but do sth. 不得不, 只好

    can’t help doing sth. 忍不住做某事

    can not help to do sth. 不能帮做某事

  6. can与be able to

    1. 表能力时, can与be able to 意义相同, 但是can仅用于一般现在时以及一般过去时, be able to 可以用于更多的时态, 可用于一般过去时、将来时和完成时。
    2. 过去形式的could与was/were able to相比, could仅说明具备某种能力, 而was/were able to则表示过去设法做成了某事, 相当于managed to do

may, might

  1. 表示请求和许可。在疑问句中, mgiht可以代替may, 语气更加委婉

  2. 表示可能性, may可能性大于might, might语气更委婉（可指现在和过去的可能性）。表示可能性时, may和might不用再问句中

  3. 用于祈使句表示祝愿

    May the force be with you !

  4. 固定结构

    may/might well + 动词原型, 表示“很可能”（相当于与probably）

    ​ He may well come here next week.

    may/might as well + 动词原形, 表“最好；不妨”

    ​ Since it is rainning hard, you may as well stay here.

must, have to（否定形式为don’t have to）

  1. must表示“必须, 应该”, have to意为“不得不”, 意义与must相近, 但must表示说话人的主观看法, 其否定形式mustn’t表示禁止做某事；have to则强调客观需要, 有人称、时态变化

    Men and women must be treated equally in education and employment.

    You mustn’t speak loudly in public.

    I have to stay at home, because it is raining.

    He had to study hard to enter a key university.

    You mustn’t smoke in the office.（禁止吸烟）

  2. must引起的疑问句做否定回答, 用needn’t或don’t have to, 意为“不必”

    ——Must I hand in my paper now ?

    ——Yes, you must. / No, you needn’t（或don’t have to）

  3. must表示非常有把握的推测, 意为“一定；准是”, 用于肯定句

    must do 对现在发生事情的推测

    must have done 对过去发生事情的推测

    must be doing 对正在发生事情的推测

    ​ The teacher must be angrey, because so many students are late.

    ​ Look at his new car. He must have earned a lot of money

  4. must表示“偏要, 非要…不可”, 暗示说话人对主语发出的动作或行为是不希望的、不满的, 甚至是生气的

    Must you make so loud noise ?

    Must you smoke in the office ?

shall（与第一人称连用表示将来, 相当于will）

  1. 用于第二、第三人称的陈述句, 表示说话人的意愿、命令、警告、允诺、威胁等

    I promised he shall get a present for his birthday.（允诺）

    You shall be punished if you break the rule.（警告）

  2. 用于第一人称、第三人称的疑问句中, 表示说话人征求对方的意见

    Shall the driver wait outside ?

    Shall I open the door ?

  3. 在条约、规定、法令等文件中表示规定或义务等, 意为“必须”, 常用第三人称。

    It has been announced that candidates shall remain in their seats until all the papers have been collected.

  4. should, ought to（should比ought to的语气轻）

    1. 表示“应该、应当”

      As a student, you should finish your homework on time.

      You ought to take care of your parents when they are old.

    2. should表示惊讶、忧虑、惋惜等情绪, 意为“竟然, 竟会”

      That he should speak to you like that is quite astonishing.

      I am surprised that you should make the same mistake agian.

    3. should表推测, 指有一定根据的推测, 意为“（按道理）应该”, 可与ought to互换

      Should have done = ought to have done 本应该做而未做

      Should not have done = ought not to have done 本应该不坐却做了

      The pictures should be ready this afternoon.

      He should be in the office at the moment.

will, would

  1. 表示意志或意愿, 否定是为won’t

    I will share my thoughts and feelings with you.

    He will not give his telephone number to a stranger.

  2. 表请求, would比will语气更委婉

    Will you mind opning th window for me ?

  3. 表示习惯性动作, “总是”；would则表示过去的习惯性动作

    would过去常常, 不能与be动词连用, 不与现在形成对比, used to do/be与现在形成对比

    Sometimes he will wander in the street all the afternoon.

    I would have lunch at this restaurant when I stayed in Beijing.

  4. would表示请求, 语气更委婉

    Would you mind my opening the window ?

need, dare

need“需要”, dare“敢”, 都既可用作情态动词（+do）, 又可用作实义动词（+to do）, （need作实义动词时, 表示“需要”, 后接名词、代词、不定式、动名词等；dare用作实义动词, 表示“敢”, 后接不定式, 在否定句和疑问句中, 不定式符号to可以省略。）做情态动词常用于疑问句、否定句和条件句, need引起的一般疑问句做否定句回答用needn’t, 肯定回答用must。

——Dare you go to the forest at night ?

——Yes, I dare./No, I dare not.

Most people hate Harry but they don’t to say so.

How dare you do that ?

——Need I give him a call now ?

——Yes, you must./No, you needn’t.

I need to go to see the doctor because of my bad cold.

used to

表示过去常发生的动作或习惯, 但现在不存在了

He used to go swimming at weekends in summer.（陈述部分含有used to的反义疑问句的附加文具用“usedn’t+主语”或“didn’t+主语”）

比较：would表示过去常发生的动作或习惯, 不强调现在。

“情态动词+have done”的用法

  1. must have done 想必/一定做过…只用于肯定句子（must表推测时可能性最大, 是有根据的判断, must表推测时用于肯定句, 不用于否定句和疑问句。否定句、疑问句中表推测用can/could）

    The ground is wet. It must have rained last night.

  2. can’t have done/couldn’t have done不可能做了…/过去没能够

    John can’t have been at home, because I saw him in the street just now.

  3. could have done 本来能够做某事（而实际未做）/可能已经做过某事

    We could have arrived at home, but we missed the last bus.

  4. may/might have done 可能已经做了…；用于否定句表示“可能不”

    He might have known the result of the match on TV.

  5. should/ought to have done 本应该做而未做…

    shouldn’t/ought not to have done 本不应该做而做了…

    I should have accepted the teacher’s advice at that time.

    You should not have said these words to your parents.

  6. needn’t have done 本不必做而做了…

    We needn’t have taken the umbrella. It has cleared up.

    ​ He needn’t have come. 他本来没必要来。（实际却来了）

    ​ He didn’t need to come. 他没有必要来。（实际也没来）

虚拟语气的用法

虚拟语气在非真实条件句中的应用（条件句可以放在主句后面）

虚拟情况 虚拟条件从句 主句
与现在事实相反的假设 If + 主语 + 动词的过去式（be动词用were） 主语 + should/would/could/might + 动词原形
与过去事实相反的假设 If + 主语 + had + 过去分词 主语 + should/would/could/might + have + 过去分词
与将来事实相反的假设 If + 主语 + 动词的过去式；If + 主语 + were to + 动词原形；If + 主语 + should（不能省略） + 动词原形 主语 + should/would/could/might + 动词原形

If I were you, I would tell him the good news.

If it should rain, the crops would be saved.

If he had won the game, he wouldn’t have been so sad.

注意

在条件句中可省略if, 且把were, should, had（必须是助动词）提到句首, 构成部分倒装（否定不用缩写）

Were I you, I would go to Beijing.

Should it rain tomorrow, we would not go camping.

Had you taken my advice, you would not have failed in the exam.

Had you not taken my advice, you would have failed in the exam.

错综时间条件句中虚拟语气的使用

当从句的动作与主句发生的时间不一致时, 主从句动词的形式要根据各自所表示的时间做出相应的调整。

If I were you, I would have taken his advice.

I would have gone to Beijing with her last week, but I was busy.

If she had studied hard（与过去事实相反） , she would be a high school student now.（与现在事实相反）

含蓄条件句中虚拟语气的使用

有时条件句并不是用if引导的从句表示, 而是通过一些词或短语表示, 如without, but for, but, otherwise, or（否则）等。

Without electricity, the world would be dark.

But for（要不是, 相当于if it were not for…）your help, we couldn’t have finished the work on time.

He was very busy yesterday, otherwise he would have come to the party.

虚拟语气在表示建议、愿望、命令、坚持等动词后的宾语从句中的使用

动词（谓语）用“should + 动词原形”, should可以省略。这些动词为：一坚持（insist）, 二命令（order, command）, 三建议（advise, suggest, propose）, 四要求（demand, deisre, request, require）

He insisted that he (should) be sent there.

I suggested that we (should) practise speaking English everyday.

注意

  1. suggest 作“建议”讲, 表示一种意见时, 其后宾语从句用虚拟语气；若作“表明, 暗示”讲, 用于陈述一种实际情况的时候, 从句用陈述语气。

    The smile on his face suggested that he was satisfied with our work.

  2. insist作“坚持要求”讲, 表示一种要求、请求时, 其后宾语从句用虚拟语气；若insist作“坚持认为”讲, 用陈述一种看法、实情时, 从句用陈述语气。

    He insisted that he hadn’t stolen the money and insisted that he should be set free at once.

It + be + suggested/requested/ordered that…

以及It + be + necessary/natural/important that…等结构的主语从句及suggestion/order/proposal/plan…等词后的表语从句或同位语从句中, 谓语动词用“should + 动词原形”

It is necessary that we (should) learn English well.

It is suggested that we (should) start before sunset.

虚拟语气在其他从句以及一些固定句式的用法

  1. wish后接宾语从句的三种形式：had done（与过去事实相反）/were或did（与现在事实相反）/would/could do（与将来事实相反）

    I wish I knew what was going to happen.

    He wishes he could become a scientist some day.

  2. 在if only（要是…就好了）引导的感叹句中, 用虚拟语气表示愿望

    If only I had been to Paris.

    注意！ 区别only if（意为“只有”, 表示条件, 当only if位于句首时, 条件句用部分倒装）

    You will becomne stronger only if you exercise every day.

  3. 主句 + as if （though） + 从句（虚拟语气, 谓语动词did/were/had done）/（陈述语气, 谓语动词用正常时态）

    as if/as though/if only + 从句, 用于虚拟语气时, 与现在事实相反的谓语用did, 与过去事实相反的用had done, 与将来事实相反的用would/could/might do

    He talked about the accident as if he had seen it.

    It is cloudy. It looks as if it is going to rain.（如果有明显迹象表示可能发生时, 不用虚拟语气）

  4. It is time + that 从句

    It is (high/about) time (that) sb. did/should do sth. 某人该做某事了

    It is high time that we should get up. = It is high time that we got up.

  5. would （rather/prefer）（that）sb. （had done…与过去事实相反）/（were/did…与现在/将来事实相反）

    I would rather that you had taken my advise.

    I would prefer that you went to Shanghai with me next week.
