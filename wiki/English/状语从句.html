状语从句

时间状语从句

when

When可以引导持续动作，也可以引导短暂动作，可用于主句和从句同时发生或从句动作先于主句动作发生，当主句是将来时，从句的时态用现在时。

When the teacher came in, Jim was drawing a horse on the blackboard.
I will go home when I finish my work.

When还可以用作并列连词，意为“在这时，在那时”，相当于and at this/that time.

be about to do… when… 正要准备做某事，这时…
be doing… when… 正在做某事，这时…
be on the point of doing… when… 正要做某事，这时…
had just done… when… 刚做完某事，这时…

while

While只引导持续动作（时间段）, 或者连接两个并列句，意为“然而”。

While I am on holiday, ring me.
Please don’t make a noise while they are sleeping.
It often rains in the south, while it seldom rains in the north.

as

As表示主从句两个动作交替进行或同时完成，译为“一边…一边…”或“随着”

As he grew older, he bacame less active.
As I got off the bus, I saw my father.

when/while/as选择

作时间状语从句引导词时：
be doing持续动作, do短暂动作

主句 引导词 从句
doing while doing
do when/while/as doing
do when/as do
doing when do

一… 就…

as soon as, the moment/minute/second/instant, no sooner…than, hardly/scarcely…when…, immediately, directly, instantly, on doing sth.
从句中用一般时态代替将来时

no sooner + 主句 + than + 从句
hardly/scarcely + 主句 + than + 从句
如果no sooner, hardly/scarcely主句部分倒装。
Hardly/Scarcely had he heard the news when he began to cry.

till, until, not… until

肯定句：主语谓语动词是延续性动词，意为“某动作一直持续到某时间点才停止”

延续性动词是指表示能够延续的动作。如：study, work, stand, lie, know, walk, keep, live,have, wait, watch, sing, read, sleep,stay等。

The waited till/until I returned.

否定句：主语谓语动词是非延续性动词（非延续性动词的否定是延续性动词），意为“某动作知道某时间开始”
He didn’t go to bed until his mother came back.

Until可以放置句首，till不可以

Not…until

强调句： It is/was not until… that…
（部分）倒装句： Not until… do(did) sb. do…
It was not until the professor came that we began the experiment.
Not until he told me about it again did I have any idea of it.

before, since

It (will be)/was + 时间段 + before从句
It (won’t be)/was + 时间段 + before从句

It is//has been + 时间段 + since从句
若从句谓语动词为非延续性动词，表示“自从……有多久”；若从句的谓语从句为延续性动词，表示“自从结束/完成……起有多久”
It is three years since I smoked. = It is three years since I stopped smoking. 我戒烟有三年了。
It is three years since I began to smoke. 我吸烟有三年了。

在时间状语从句中，不能用一般将来时或过去将来时，而要用一般现在时，或一般过去时代替将来时。

地点状语

表抽象条件时，从句必须放在主句之前。
Where there is water, there is life. 有水的地方就有生命。
Where there is a will, there is a way. 有志者事竟成。

原因状语从句

because 因为——必然因果关系，用来回答why。
The old man asked Lucy to move to another chiar because he wanted to sit next to his wife.

since 既然，由于——语气稍弱，表水对方已经知晓的原因。
Since everyone is here, we can begin our discussion.

as 语气最弱，往往是不明显的原因，只是对结果的附带说明。
I can;t go with you, as I have a lot of work to do.

considering (that), seeing (that), now (that)和since意义相似，都有鉴于某个事实的意思。
Now that everyone is here, let’s start our meeting.

for表原因时作并列连词，连词的分句表示原因的一些辅助性的补充说明。
He could not have seem me, for I was not there.

目的状语从句

  1. in order that, so that表示“以便，为了”，他们引导的状语从句经常与情态动词can, may, could, might连用。so that必须置于主句之后。
    I’ll speaking slowly so thst you can make sense of what I said.
    The plice officers in our city work hard in order that we can live a safe life.
  2. for fear that/in case 害怕，担心/以防万一
    The boy hid himself behind the tree for fear thst his father might see him.
    Take your umbrella in case it should rain.
    in case of 是介词，意为“假使，如果”。

结果状语从句

if, unless(=if…not…), so/as long as, in case, on condition that, suppose/supposing that, provided/providing that

方式状语从句(as, as if/though)

as if/though引导的从句一般用虚拟语气，但若从句中陈述的事实很可能实现，也用陈述语气。
Do as you are told to.
Jack wasn’t saying anything, but the teacher smiled at him as if he had done something very clever.（虚拟）
I feel as if I have a cold.（陈述）

让步状语

though/although, even if/though(假设语境时用虚拟语气)
Even if I were in your place, I wouldn’t take the job.(虚拟语气)

  1. as/though倒装（although不能倒装）
    adj./adv./无冠词名词/动词原形 + as/though + 主 + (谓语)，主句
    Pop star as she is, she still needs improve.
    Try as he may, he never succeeds.
    Cold as it is, his father goes out to work every day.
    Much as I have traveled, I have never seem anyone who’s as capable as John.
  2. no matter wh-/wh- (略)
  3. when, while做从属连词，相当于although
    While I know English is important, I am not interested in it.

比较状语从句

as…as, not as(so)…as…, more…than…, less…than…, (the more…,the more… ), the same…as…

状语从句中的省略

  1. 如果状语从句中的主语和主句中的主语一致，为了避免重复，常把后面的动词省略，从句中的主语和be可省略。
    Unless asked, I will not say anything.
    When sleeping, you should turn off the TV set.
    While in America, I learned a lot about American customs.
    My stepmother treats me ai if my own mother.
    When young, I liked music very much.

  2. 如果状语从句中的主语是it, 谓语是be, 或从剧中是there be句型时, 常把it和be或there be省略
    I will give a call before I arrive in Beijing if possible.

    Please correct my mistakes if any.(=if there is any mistake) —— if any 若有的话
    The island is seldom, if ever, visited by ships.(=if the island has ever been visited by ships) —— if ever 如果有过的话(如果发生过的话)
    He is only too ready to help others,seldom, if ever, refusing them when they turn to him.(他非常愿意帮助别人，如果他曾经拒绝过帮助别人，那也是很少的。)
    if so 如果这样, if never 如果从来没有, if necessary 如果有必要
