主谓一致（简略）

dozen/score

dozen：一打=12个

score：20个

  1. 单数形式：two/many/serval dozen/score books
  2. 复数形式：dozens/scores of + 复数名词——许多

语法一致

主语为单数形式时，谓语动词使用单数形式；主语为复数形式，谓语动词使用复数。

He is a good man.

They come from China.

意义一致

指的是谓语动词的单复数取决于主语表达的具体意义，而不是取决于表面上的形式。

The committee is/are discussing the plan inthe room.

Ten years is a long time for me. d

表示时间、价格、重量、数目长度、数学运算等词或短语作主语时，常看作一整体，谓语动词用单数。

What we need is time.

What we need are ten computers.

The old in China live a happy life. ( 表示一类人 )

Both Tom and Peter are music-lovers.

The worker and the writer ( 工人和作家 ) are from Beijing.

The worker and writer ( 工人兼作家 ) is from Beijing.

His family ( 家族 ) is large.

His family ( 家人 ) are fond of music.

John is the only one of the students who has the keys.

John is one of students who have the keys.

就近一致

指的是主语有两个或两个以上的名词或代词构成，谓语动词的单数形式应与紧邻的名词或代词表示一致。

There are three books and a ruler on the desk.

Not only you but also she is a Chinese.

Many a tree was cut down. = Many trees were cut down.

More than one student is late because of the heavy rain.

Each/Every book and ( each/every ) pen is found in its place.

One or two students were cleaning the window yesterday afternoon.

There is plenty of water in the cup.

There are plenty of apples in the box.

特例（确定主语）

  1. "a/an + 单数名词 + or two"作主语，谓语动词用单数形式。

    A student or two has failed the exam.

  2. The teacher with a number of students is in the classroom. ( with仅充当修饰作用 )

  3. A number of 和 the number of

    A number of books have been sent to the children. ( 大量的书 )

    The number of books in our school is large. ( 书的数量 )

  4. This pair of trousers is very dirty. ( 用量词修饰时谓语动词和量词保持一致 )

  5. A quantity of money has been spent./Quantities of money have been spent.
